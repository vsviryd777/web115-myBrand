CATS CLUB in Charlotte

My Brand Introduction

“The cat has absolute emotional honesty: people, for one reason or another, can hide their feelings, but the cat cannot.” - Ernest Hemingway

If you live in Charlotte and are a cat person or want to learn more about them, we have collected valuable and exciting information on our website. Join our club.

My site is for those who love cats. The site will contain not only interesting information but also useful information for cat persons in our city of Charlotte.

UI/UX Design

Colors

The main color I choose is light blue (this is a symbol of stability and reliability). I will also add a dark blue color for contrast. To attract attention, I will use bright colors (yellow and orange)

Typography

For headings, I will use "Sacramento", in italics. (For beauty) For the main text part I will use the font family: 'Merriweather', serif. (For ease of reading)

UI Fundaments

  • Simple design helping our users achieve their goals faster and more efficiently.
  • Consistency on the website help our users easily move from page to page and always know where they are.
